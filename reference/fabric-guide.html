Fabric Guide

A quick-reference guide to common fabrics, their properties, and which ones are best for beginners.

Tip: Print this page as a shopping reference before buying fabric.

🌟 Best for Beginners

Start with 100% cotton quilting fabric or a cotton-poly blend. These woven fabrics don't slip while cutting, hold pins well, press beautifully, and forgive mistakes. Avoid polyester, silk, and knits until you're comfortable with the basics.
